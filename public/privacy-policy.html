Privacy Policy

Sustain-U

EcoClub UAlberta  ·  Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Sustain-U is a campus sustainability app built by EcoClub UAlberta to help University of Alberta students track their carbon footprint, manage eco-anxiety, and connect with climate wellness resources.

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Data We Do Not Collect

Sustain-U does not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, age, location)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
  • Cookies or tracking pixels
  • Camera or microphone data (the barcode scanner uses the camera only while the scanner is open — no images are stored or transmitted)

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The app saves the following data exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your phone:

  • CO₂ tracker entries and profile settings
  • Eco-anxiety check-in responses
  • Gratitude journal notes
  • Barcode scan history
  • Wellness notebook reflections

No account or login is required. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all locally stored data.

Third-Party Services

Sustain-U uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenStreetMap — The campus sustainability map loads map tiles from openstreetmap.org. When the map is viewed, your device makes requests to OpenStreetMap servers to load map imagery. OpenStreetMap's own privacy policy applies to these requests.
  • Open Food Facts — When you scan a product barcode, the barcode number is sent to the Open Food Facts public API to retrieve product information. No personal data is included in this request. Open Food Facts' terms of use apply.
  • Leaflet.js — The map uses Leaflet, an open-source JavaScript library, loaded from unpkg.com.

No other third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

No Account Required

Sustain-U requires no account creation, email address, or login of any kind. You can use all features of the app anonymously.

Children's Privacy

Sustain-U does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app is safe for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
